Dashboard : After Login through admin, you can see a Dashboard in front of your screen this is a Dashboard/Home Page of “Weapon Detection|Client”, where you can navigate through the history of the cams of all clients.

Add Cam : You can add the new cam into your Live cam section using Add cam by giving the details like protocol,ip address,username,password etc.

Live Cam : You will see your camera’s live detection in the live cam section. If you have multiple cameras you can choose which ones live detection you want to see by choosing a camera or camera number.

History : In History Section you see a table, where you can see your cam history and picture detected by the camera system (weapon detection system). The table contains the date, time, cam no. (Camera Number), picture.

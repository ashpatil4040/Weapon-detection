Dashboard : After Login through admin, you can see a Dashboard in front of your screen this is a Dashboard/Home Page of “Weapon Detection|Admin”, where you can navigate through the history of the cams of all clients.

Detection History : You can see camera history of clients and picture detected by the camera system in the History Section. The table contains the client's email, date, time, cam no. (Camera Number), picture.

Client List : You can see the lists of the clients in the Client Lits Section. The table contains the client's email, client's Name, No of camera added by client, Total no of times weapon Detected.
